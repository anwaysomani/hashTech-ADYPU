Team Description
A team may consist of a maximum of four participants, Students from different institutes can form a team.
Rules:
  1. The commpetition will be held in two rounds.
  2. The first round will be the elimination round.
    • The number of eliminations during a round will depend upon the number of entries.
    • Qualifying robots would be decided on basis of checkpoint covered or time taken to complete the track.
  3. The second round will be the final round.
    • The robot should take the minimum time to complete the track.
    • It has to stop in the finish box.
  5. each checkpoint will consist of 20 points.
  6. The robot to complete the track in minimum time will be qualified.
  7. The robot should stop automatically inside the Finish Box in Round 1 and Round 2.
  8. If above rule is satisfied by robot, it will score EXTRA 20 points.
  9. You will be provided with dry run of 1 minute (points covered in dry run will not be considered).
  10. After the dry run, you will be provided 1 minute of Calibration time. In this time, you may change your code on robot.
  11. If you don’t wish to take the Calibration time, you will be awarded with 5 bonus points.
  12. The Organizers have the right to Disqualify any participation indulging in malpractices. The decision of the Event Organizers is final and cannot be debated on.
  13. If robot deviates from its path, you may take a hand touch. Each hand touch would:
    • Add 5 second to total time.
    • After 3rd hand touch, the robot has to start from next checkpoint with penalties.
Robot Specification
  1. The robot should fit in a cube of 20cm x 20cm x 15cm.
  2. The robot should be within 2/3rd kg in weight.
  3. If the robot covers the whole track within 1 minutes(Round 1) and 2 minutes(Round 2) then it will score EXTRA 20 points.
  4. The robot must have ‘on board’ power supply.
  5. When using the electric power supply, the potential difference must not exceed 24V.
    6. The robot cannot be constructed using readymade kits or any readymade mechanisms. This leads to disqualification.
Arena Specification
  • The width of track will be 3cm.
  • The track will BLACK on white background.
  • Track for Round 1 and 2 would be disclosed during the commencement of competition.
  • Photography of arena is prohibited.
  • No open disclosure of arena.
  • Track consists of acute angles, right angles, curves and many more surprises.
    • Certification Policy
    1. Certificate of appreciation/participation will be awarded to all teams.
    2. Certificate of excellence and momentous will be given to top three teams.
    3. Disqualified team will not be considered for any certificates.
