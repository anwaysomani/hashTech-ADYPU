About Nexus Motorsport
The Nexus Motorsport, India’s leading company provides platform from children to graduates by offering wide opportunities in the motorsport field. From racing & commercial Go-Kart, Off road buggies, ATV to Formula student race teams across India rely on Nexus Motorsport’s innovative technology and first-class service. Spectacular sports victories underline the technical competence and reliability of Nexus motorsport’s products and services.
Vision
At Nexus Motorsport, we intend to provide our young emerging undergraduates with the best learning experience, where student after graduation will be a technically developed, dedicated towards own dreams and concepts, innovative and skilled engineer. We aims to build an engineer who will spend each second of his life in developing himself. We strive hard to ensure that each student in our institute evolves to their best potential.
