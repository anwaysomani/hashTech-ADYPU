Beat-My-Code
Beat-My-Code is a coder's playground to the amazingly challenging questions and competing environment, where you race yourself with the similar-grade experts to find the solution to common challenging questions. It is a mixture of 4 different queestions, varying the field of data structures, greedy algorithms, dynamic programming, and much more...
Find yourself in an exciting adventure on 28th & 29th March....


About the Challenge
Beat-My-Code is a two hour challenge, in which all the participants will be provided with 4 different programming questions, which needs to be solved in any language they wish to from the list available at: http://www.hashtech-adypu.com/Hackathon/Supported-PLs.
The question patterns are as follows...
  • Q1 - Easy Programming Question
  • Q2 - Easy Programming Question
  • Q3 - Medium Programming Question
  • Q4 - Hard Programming Question

    • * Please Note:
    - All questions are compulsary.
    - No extra time will be given to attempt the questions.
    - Particpants are expected to solve the questions in the given duration only.
    - The test environment will be closed automatically after the timer for the challenge has exhausted.

    Judgement Criteria
  • You will be ranked on the basis of score you get.
  • There are no penalties for wrong submissions, so you can submit solutions as many times as you'd like.
  • However, only your best correct submission will be considered.
  • Ties are broken according to the times each participant reached that score.
