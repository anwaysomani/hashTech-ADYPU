Team Description
A team may consist of a maximum of three participants, Students from different institutes can form a team.
Rules:
  1. The competition will be carried out in 2 rounds.
  2. Round 1:
    • Teams need to submit a PPT/PDF file of the poster highlighting the features of their working model.
    • Points to be considered while preparing the document:-
      • Problem statement being considered
      • Solution
      • Short-Market study
      • Summary of marketing strategy
      • Use cases
      • Uniqueness of Idea
        • Shortlisted teams from Round-1 would proceed to Round-2.
      • Round 2:
        • Qualified teams will present their ideas to panel of judges.
        • Suggested points to be covered in presentation:-
          • Problem Statement
          • Solution
          • How your product is different from the existing ones (if any).
          • Market Study
          • Implementation Plan
          • Marketing Strategy
          • Targeted Customer
          • Total Costing/Sponsorship required
          • Bonus Point:
            • Inverstment Proposal with return plans.
      • A maximum of 15 minutes of time would be allotted to each team which will be bifurcated as â€“ 10 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for Q&A.
      • The documents for both the rounds need to be submitted before deadline to: hashtech@adypu.edu.in
Judging Criteria
  • Innovation
  • Pecularity
  • Presentation Skills
  • Convincing Skills
  • Feasibility
Certification Policy
  1. Certificate of appreciation/participation will be awarded to all teams.
  2. Certificate of excellence and momentous will be given to top three teams.
  3. Disqualified team will not be considered for any certificates.
