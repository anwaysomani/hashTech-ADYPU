Team Description
A team may consist of a maximum of four participants, Students from different institutes can form a team.
Rules:
  1. The competition will be carried out in 2 rounds if the number of team registration exceed 20. In case of less than 20 team registrations, only round 2 will be carried out.
  2. Round 1:
    • Teams need to submit a PDF file of the poster highlighting the features of their working model.
    • The poster has to be precise in detailing the features but should not contain huge paragraphs.
    • The poster need to be sent to hashtech@adypu.edu.in
    • Shortlisted teams Round-1 would qualify to Round-2.
  3. Round 2:
    • Qualified teams will present their working models.
    • The poster to be presented should be of A1 size.
Judging Criteria
  • Innovation
  • Creativity
  • Novelty
  • Slution toward problem
Certification Policy
  1. Certificate of appreciation/participation will be awarded to all teams.
  2. Certificate of excellence and momentous will be given to top three teams.
  3. Disqualified team will not be considered for any certificates.
