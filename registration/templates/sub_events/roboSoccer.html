Team Description
A team may consist of a maximum of four participants, Students from different institutes can form a team.
Rules:
  1. The first round will be the elimination round.
    • The number of eliminations during a round will depend upon the number of entries.
    • The robot has to score maximum goals and gain points
    • Scoring Strategy:
      1. Scoring goal from behind the half line will give you 5 points.
      3. Scoring a goal from outside the "D" area will give you 3 points.
      5. Scroring goal from inside the "D" area willl give you 3 points.
      6. While pushing the ball into the goal, if the bot crosses the goal line, it would score 1 point.
  2. Next Rounds:
    • The level of difficulty may increase by qualifying the rounds.
    • There is no point system after elimination round.
    • Team scoring maximum goals would be declared winner.
  3. During the rounds, only maximum of two members are allowed in the arena.
  4. Stretching of the wire by the wireman may lead to disqualification.
  5. For wired robot, length of the wires must be sufficient enough to cover the arena.
  6. No time will be given once the robot is on the arena. If the bot is proved to be inactive for more than 60 seconds, it is disqualified.
  7. If the robot of the particpant's is not working on the spot, they can use the organizer's robot.
  8. Any of the rules can be changed by the coordinators before the commencement of competition, and particpants would be notified/informed about it 30 minutes before the competition.
  9. Final scoring and penalties rules will be announced at the day of the event.
  10. The organizers have the right to disqualify any participation indulging in malpractices. The decision of the event organizer's is final and cannot be debated upon.
  11. The robot should not damage the arena. It may lead to disqualification and fine of certain amount of money.
Robot Specification
  1. The bot must fit into a cube of 300mm X 300mm X 300mm(lxbxh) and must weigh less than or equal to 3 kg.
  2. The battery used cannot exceed 12 V with max load current of 4 amp. The power supply may or may not be onboard. Participants should bring their own power supply. No bench supply would be provided by the organizers.
  3. The robot must not leave behind any of its parts during the run, else it will result in disqualification.
Certification Policy
  1. Certificate of appreciation/participation will be awarded to all teams.
  2. Certificate of excellence and momentous will be given to top three teams.
  3. Disqualified team will not be considered for any certificates.
